Troubleshooting & FAQ

Port 8680 masih digunakan oleh instalasi lama (code-server). Matikan dulu:

systemctl stop code-server@root
systemctl restart vscode-server
  1. Stop service lama: systemctl stop code-server@root
  2. Disable service lama: systemctl disable code-server@root
  3. Jalankan script instalasi Official yang baru.
  4. Login ulang extension di VS Code baru.
Status: systemctl status vscode-server
Logs: journalctl -u vscode-server -f
Restart: systemctl restart vscode-server
